


## Working Agreement:
**Culture/Values**
- We self-assign tasks to promote autonomy
- We share work early and often even when it’s unfinished, to stay transparent
- We solve problems together without assigning blame, to foster growth
- We celebrate wins, big or small
- We revisit and improve this agreement as needed

**Communication**
- Check and use discord daily to stay up to date
- Aim to respond to team pings within a working day
- Share your availabilities at the start of each sprint
- Use /standup whenever starting any type of work
- Proactively ask for help when stuck, and offer help when available

**Collaboration**
- Stick to one task at a time
- Prioritize reviewing PRs over progressing on assigned tasks, unblocking the team comes first
- No unplanned work should be added mid-sprint unless the team agrees
- Use PRs and pairing sessions as opportunities to learn and share knowledge
